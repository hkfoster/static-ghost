Node Knockout 2013

November 8th, 2013

The Node Knockout 2013 hackathon is taking place this weekend, and we’re really excited to be part of it. We’ll be providing hosting for the weekend plus some prizes for the winners.

We’ve been part of Node Knockout for several years now, and each year we continue to be astounded by the amazing things that people can come up with in just one weekend! We’re also impressed by the growth of the Node community, and Node Knockout is a great way to see this, with so many talented and passionate people getting involved.

Last year, we saw some great projects that included: a tool to tweet payments to your friends; a game of knights and narwhals, oh, and a christmas sweater that receives messages from the internet! We can’t wait to see what the competitors come up with this year.

The hackathon will last for 48 hours, starting at 12:00 AM UTC time on November 9. Winners will be announced on November 18, so make sure you head over to the Node Knockout Blog to see what the winners have created.

Best of luck to all those competing!
