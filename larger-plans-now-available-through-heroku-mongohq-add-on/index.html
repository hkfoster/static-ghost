Larger Plans Now Available Through Heroku MongoHQ Add-On

June 7th, 2010

It’s been a little over a month since we announced the MongoHQ Heroku Add-On and in that time we have seen some good traction from the Heroku community.

The beta period on the Heroku platform has been a great success, so we are announcing that we are ending our time of testing and are making all four types of MongoHQ databases available through the MongoHQ Add-On.

The best part? Adding a database to your Heroku application is still as simple as:

heroku addons:add mongohq:small

Running your Mongo databases through Heroku, in our minds, is one of the simplest ways to manage an application. The ease of scalability on both the application and data level is amazing.

We can’t wait to see what you can do!
