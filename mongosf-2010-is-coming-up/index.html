MongoSF 2010 Is Coming Up

April 13th, 2010

We are excited to have the opportunity to take part in and present at MongoSF in San Francisco at the end of April. Besides being put on by 10gen and having a stellar cast of contributors, it will be packed with updated information about the latest in document database technology and will include numerous walk-throughs and examples of real-world uses of MongoDB.

During our talk, we plan to present our vision for MongoHQ as well as the future of document databases as a service. Over the last couple of months, we have been working on and preparing new features and an updated approach to our offerings. So, along with sharing that information, we will take some time to walk through a MongoHQ implementation, discuss a few challenges we have overcome and will include some helpful tips that we can pass along.

One last note…we plan to use this time to make a special announcement regarding a partnership we have been working to foster over the last few months with an amazing company. It should be fun.

Even though the event is now sold out, there is a waiting list. If you are already signed up, we are looking forward to seeing you there!
