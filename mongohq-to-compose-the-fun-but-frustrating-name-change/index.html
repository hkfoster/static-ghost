Project ChuckleDuck - How MongoHQ became Compose

August 8th, 2014

*MongoHQ is now Compose – Learn More

We had a name, we had a logo, then it was time to have fun with it. Our team began playing with the logo and mashing it up with other concepts. DJ, our content curator, created space invaders with the logo, Brandon, an awesome designer here at Compose, threw in some suggestions, I tweaked the invaders a bit and bam, we had our banner image for social media and this post.

A name and brand change is not something that just happens on a whim, or at least it shouldn’t be. Like everything else here at Compose, it was a team effort, open and engaged the entire company in the search. A great new name will go well with all the great things we have planned and everyone here knows they were a part of selecting it. Because thats how we do it at Compose.
