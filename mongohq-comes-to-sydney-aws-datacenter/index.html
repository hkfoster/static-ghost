MongoHQ Comes to Sydney AWS Datacenter

November 15th, 2012

On the heels of the 10gen’s MongoDB Sydney conference, AWS announced a new datacenter: ap-southeast-2.

With a few tweaks of code, we were ready with Sydney deployments hours after AWS made their announcement. We are excited to bring MongoDB and the MongoHQ service to another continent. To read more about the AWS announment, see Announcing the AWS Asia Pacific (Sydney) Region.

For any questions about the MongoHQ offering in Sydney, please email support@mongohq.com, and we will respond promptly.
